    I'm a designer and creative technologist.

    I’m currently based in Brooklyn and finishing up my thesis and final year at Parsons, majoring in Design and Technology with a minor in Communication Design. My interests lie in creative coding, quantum computing, and immersive environments. In my work, I specialize in web development, interaction design, and data-driven visualizations.

    Core Values

    Welcome inspiration.

    My goal is to use interactive design to connect people in a compelling way. I look for inspiration from observation, conversation, and formal design research to stretch my perspective.

    Be empathetic.

    I consider how my product will intervene in the system its a part of, as well as how it will affect the people within that system. I remember that others experience the world differently and ask how I can make my products most acessible.

    Embrace experimentation.

    I strive to branch out in my work and make new discoveries with every step of the design process. I try to always stay curious and allow myself to learn new and more meaningful ways to solve problems that help me turn my ideas into real experiences.

    Build relationships.

    Collaboration and teamwork is essential for good design. By taking the time to build relationships with collaborators, I'm able to create solutions efficiently and effectively.

    Have any thoughts, comments or ideas? Let’s connect!
